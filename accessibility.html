Accessibility Statement

Tesla Motors Ltd. ("Tesla") is committed to making its website accessible to everyone, including people with disabilities. To this end, Tesla works and invests many resources on an ongoing basis to improve the accessibility of its website and its content, in order to ensure equal access to all surfers the site. The website has been adjusted according to the regulations on equal rights for people with disabilities (accessibility adjustments to the service), the recommendations of the Israeli standard (TI 5568) and the W3C's Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.1 standard.

Accessibility fixes and adjustments

Tesla's goal is to support a wide variety of web browsers and assistive technologies. Therefore, efforts have been made to support software with a wide market share, including Windows, IOS, and Android and the prominent web browsers such as Chrome and Explorer. Web content technologies we have used: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, JPG, SVG, PNG, PDF, MP4. In addition, below are the adjustments made as part of the site's accessibility and the options available in it:

  • The site has text alternatives for any non-text content, such as images or icons.
  • Information, structure and relationships conveyed through a presentation can be recognized by assistive software.
  • Color is not used as the only visual means of conveying information, and contrast-ratio has been improved for visually impaired users.
  • You can navigate the content provided on the site using keys and keyboard shortcuts.
  • The content provided on the site has headings that describe a topic or purpose, and a user can bypass repetitive blocks on the pages of the site.
  • The default human language of the content on the site can be detected by software.
  • Enabling ARIA features, unique homepage identifiers on investor relations websites and product websites.
  • You can navigate with the keyboard by choosing a color and wheels in interactive "carousel" elements on the site.

Contact

Despite our efforts to make the site accessible to people with disabilities, it is possible that certain pages on the site have not yet been accessed, including for the reason that an adequate technological solution for accessibility has not been found, and it is also possible that certain pages on the site may contain human errors due to a variety of reasons. We are committed to continuing to improve the accessibility of our sites. For inquiries and questions about accessibility, you can contact the accessibility coordinator, Nitai Gilboa at ngilboa@tesla.com or at 03-3751760.
